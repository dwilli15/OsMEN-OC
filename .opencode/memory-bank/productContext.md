# Product Context

Multi-agent AI orchestration platform. Single-model AI cannot self-correct.
Structured teams with defined accountability, adversarial review, and human
oversight at critical transitions solve the sycophancy problem.

## Two Modes

- **Mode A (cooperative)**: Structured team execution. Roles: steward, planner,
  researcher, worker, critic, verifier.
- **Mode B (adversarial)**: Structured debate. Roles: proposer, naysayer,
  lateral_innovation, stable_focus, iteration_steward, verifier. Max 3 cycles
  before human escalation.

## Swarm-Elite Hierarchy

Small NPU models (0.6B-4B) handle 90% of calls. Cloud frontier models handle
the remaining 10% (strategic synthesis, arbitration, final answers).

## UX Principles

- Discord is a view and input channel, not the state store.
- Agent public speech is scarce. Anti-storm controls are mechanical, not prompted.
- Humans approve critical transitions. If unavailable, workflows downgrade to advisory-only.
